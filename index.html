FAQs

Commercial waste questions, answered

Everything you need to know about setting up business waste collection with Stratos Environmental.

How much does commercial waste collection cost?

Prices depend on the type of waste, bin size and how often you need collections. We'll give you a free, no-obligation quote tailored to your business — just tell us your needs and we'll find you the best price.

What types of business waste do you collect?

We handle general waste, dry mixed recycling, food waste, paper & cardboard, glass, and clinical/healthcare waste. If you have multiple waste streams, we can arrange them all under one simple contract.

How quickly can you set up my collections?

Often the same day. Once you're happy with your quote we arrange bin delivery and schedule your first collection right away, so there's minimal disruption to your business.

Which areas of the UK do you cover?

We're headquartered in London and operate nationwide through our trusted partner network — covering England, Scotland, Wales and Northern Ireland.

Do you provide the bins and containers?

Yes. We supply the right bins or containers for your waste — from 240L wheelie bins up to 1100L containers, FEL/REL bins and skips — and deliver them as part of your setup.

Are your services compliant with waste regulations?

Absolutely. All collections are fully Duty of Care compliant and carried out by registered waste carriers, and you'll receive the documentation you need for your records.
