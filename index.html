FAQs

Commercial waste questions, answered

Everything you need to know about comparing commercial waste providers with Stratos Environmental.

Are you a waste collection company?

No — we're a commercial waste broker. We compare quotes from our network of licensed collection partners and help you find the best deal. Collections are carried out by registered waste carriers, not by us directly.

How much does commercial waste collection cost?

Prices depend on waste type, bin size and collection frequency. Share your details or upload your current invoice and we'll compare providers to find you the best price — free and no obligation.

What types of business waste can you compare?

General waste, dry mixed recycling, food waste, paper & cardboard, glass, and clinical/healthcare waste. We can compare multiple waste streams under one contract.

How quickly can collections be set up?

Often the same day. Once you've chosen your preferred quote, our partner arranges bin delivery and your first collection with minimal disruption.

Can you beat my current waste bill?

Very often, yes. Upload your current waste invoice and we'll compare it against our network of trusted suppliers to see if we can save you money.

Are collections compliant with waste regulations?

Yes. All collections are arranged through registered waste carriers and are fully Duty of Care compliant, with the documentation you need.
